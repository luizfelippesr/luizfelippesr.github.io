Astro-lunch

The astro-lunch is a very informal regular seminar/journal-club of the Applied Maths and Physics groups of the School of Mathematics, Statistics & Physics at Newcastle University.

The contributions can be in the form of talks or discussions about any subject relevant to current astrophysical research.

The astro-lunch meetings are held every Wednesday at the Large Meeting Room or Library, Level 3, Herschel Building.

To subscribe the mailing list and/or to reserve a slot, please contact Luiz or Amit.
