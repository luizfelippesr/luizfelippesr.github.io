I am a Research Associate at School of Mathematics, Statistics and Physics at Newcastle University.

My main research interests are:

  • the role played by cosmic rays, magnetic fields and turbulence on the star formation in galaxies;
  • the physical properties of the interstellar medium;
  • the evolution of galactic magnetic fields, and
  • computational modelling of galaxy formation and evolution.
