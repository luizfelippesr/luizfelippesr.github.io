Profile

I am a data scientist and computational astrophysicist working as Research Associate at School of Mathematics, Statistics and Physics at Newcastle University.

My main academic research interests are:

  • the role played by cosmic rays and magnetic fields on galaxy formation and evolution;
  • the evolution of galactic magnetic fields, and
  • the physical properties of the interstellar medium
