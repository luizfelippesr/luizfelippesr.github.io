Science,
This strange and noisy dialogue with Nature,
this feverish inquiry of every little aspect of the so-called Reality,
inexhaustible source of wonder and beauty.

However, it cannot be seen as anything more than small part of the human quest for answers and meaning. This is why I am also heavily interested in Art (particularly poetry and music) the alternate human way to explore the world and to search for meaning, by probing the deepness of our feelings and experiences.

When there is some time, I also enjoy studying the rather sofisticated techniques that were employed by our ancestors to defend themselves (with swords) in the violent streets of the renaissance epoch.
